Claude Hotmail Connector — what this is, and why it's safe
============================================================

This installs a small helper that lets Claude read your email and read/write your
calendar in a PERSONAL Microsoft account (hotmail.com / outlook.com / live.com).

  • It runs entirely on THIS computer. There is no server and no cloud account —
    Claude talks to it locally, and it talks straight to Microsoft on your behalf.
  • You sign in with Microsoft's own sign-in page. This app never sees your
    password, and your sign-in is stored ENCRYPTED on this PC only.
  • Least privilege: it can READ mail and read/write your calendar. It cannot send
    mail and has no delete tool.
  • The source code is open: https://github.com/TGoodhew/Claude-Hotmail-Connector

A note about the Windows warning
--------------------------------
This installer is not code-signed (a certificate no longer removes the warning for
low-volume apps, so it isn't worth the cost). Windows may show a blue
"Windows protected your PC" screen. To continue, click "More info", then
"Run anyway". You can verify what you downloaded against the checksums on the
project's Releases page.
